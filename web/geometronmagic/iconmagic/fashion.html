FASHION

Trash Magic fashion is self-replicating media like the other elements of Trash Magic. We find clothes and other textile products in waste streams, clean them, sew them together, and transform them into clothes which transmit the idea of Trash Magic using the same design ethos as on the Trash Magic web: maximalist rainbow colors on a black background.

Items made can be photographed with labels and the photos put in a folder /var/www/html/media/fashion, with those photos listed here.
